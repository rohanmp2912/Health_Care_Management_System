Orthopedic

Orthopedics, or orthopaedics, is a medical specialty that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, prevention, and rehabilitation of musculoskeletal conditions. The musculoskeletal system includes bones, joints, muscles, ligaments, tendons, and other connective tissues. Orthopedic physicians, commonly known as orthopedic surgeons or orthopedists, specialize in managing a broad range of orthopedic issues.
